About Us

How it all began

It all started back in 1995,right after the war ended.Florists weren't present in Bosnia at that time so we decided to be the pioneers

We started with simple pelargonium and now,we grow from Orchidea to Chilli peppers.

Location

Our production and shop are both located in Mrakovo,Ilijaš,just a few minutes of driving from Konzum
